üîç Data We Collect & Process

Shabari follows a minimal data collection approach. The app processes certain data locally on your device based on your manual actions:

Information You Manually Choose to Scan (Processed Locally)

  • SMS Messages: When you use the SMS Scanner, you manually select messages to analyze. Analysis happens on your device. Message content is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Files and Photos: When you choose "Scan File" or use "Share to Shabari", the file is processed on your device. For enhanced threat detection, only an anonymized file signature (hash) may be sent to VirusTotal. The actual file content is never uploaded.
  • URLs/Links: When you scan a URL manually or from a QR code, the URL is checked against Google Safe Browsing and VirusTotal for threat verification.
  • QR Codes: When you point the camera at a QR code, the decoded content is analyzed for security threats. No images are stored.

Account Information (Required for App Functionality)

  • Email Address: Used exclusively for account creation and authentication
  • Subscription Status: Premium or free tier status to enable appropriate features
  • Account Creation Date: For account management and support purposes
  • App Settings: Your preferences and feature configurations

Optional Analytics (You Can Disable)

  • App Usage Statistics: Which features are used (no personal content or scan results)
  • Error Reports: Crash logs for app improvement (no personal data included)
  • Performance Metrics: App performance data (completely anonymous)
